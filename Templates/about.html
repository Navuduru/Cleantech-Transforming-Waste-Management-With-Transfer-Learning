About Poultry Detect

Poultry Detect is your intelligent assistant for detecting common poultry diseases using deep learning. Our mission is to help farmers and poultry keepers quickly identify diseases like Coccidiosis and take timely action to prevent outbreaks and reduce economic loss. With our image-based prediction system, poultry health diagnosis becomes easier and more efficient.
